The Basics

What is Green Energy?

A cleaner more sustainable way
of living.

Green energy is an energy generated from natural sources without harming the natural environment. These sources are often renewable meaning they are repleshined naturally and will not run out. Homeowners have many options when it comes to supplying their home with green power, with the 2 most common being Green Pricing & Self Supply.

What is Green Pricing?

Simple. Affordable.
Quick.

Many homeowners have the option to opt in for green power for their electricity. Referred to as green pricing or utility green power products these are offered directly through your utility provider. Some homeowners live in de-regulated electricity markets. In these markets you have the option to purchase green energy from an alternative provider other than your default utility provider. Learn more about what Green Pricing is & state specific info here

Whar is Self Supply?

For those who live
DIY.

As the name implies self-supply is for households who want to provide their own green power. From solar panels to geothermal plants there's many options. Learn more about the self supply options that are available to you here.
