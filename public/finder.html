Energy Star Product Finder

The Navix Energy Star Product Finder tool lets you find products across three separate categories that are certified Energy Star products. Energy Star products are energy-efficient appliances and devices that meet strict guidelines set by the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA). These products help reduce energy consumption and greenhouse gas emissions, resulting in cost savings on your utility bills and a lower environmental impact.
